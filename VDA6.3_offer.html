Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the engagement for a VDA 6.3:2023 Process Audit at Stokvis Celix Portugal (Porto / Braga district, Portugal), requested via Pro QC International Ltd.

The audit is expected to require approximately 1 man-day (MD) on-site and is designed to evaluate process capability, identify non-conformities and improvement opportunities, and provide a clear report aligned with the VDA 6.3:2023 standard.

The audit will be conducted using the official VDA 6.3 audit tool (VDA 6.3 Process Auditor). Any specific customer process audit requirements — such as VW supply chain special assessments — must be identified and communicated prior to the audit to ensure proper scope alignment.

3. Audit Scope

For a 1 MD on-site audit, it is highly recommended to audit the scope P5 to P7:

  • P5 — Supplier Management
  • P6 — Process Analysis / Production
  • P7 — Customer Support / Customer Satisfaction

The specific processes and sub-elements to be audited will be confirmed during the preparation phase in alignment with Pro QC and the client's requirements.
