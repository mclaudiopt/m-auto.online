¶ PIWIS III 特性:

New operation system and more intuitive diagnostic interface

New VCI for post-2017+ vehicles with DoIP diagnosis

Good at maintenance, repairing, control units changing and programming & setting codes

Can login for online immobilizer programming if you have online certificate & account

Can find out leading faults

Can look for maintenance wiring diagram

Support special functions like modification or flashing, with Engineering mode (Development version)
