Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the engagement for an internal audit of your IATF16949:2016 Quality Management System. The audit is designed to evaluate compliance with the standard, identify areas of non-conformity and opportunities for improvement, and provide clear recommendations to strengthen system performance. The objective is to enhance process effectiveness, support continuous improvement initiatives, and support readiness for external assessments or certifications.

1. Auditor Profile

Senior Quality Professional with 26 years of experience in the multinational automotive industry, including quality systems, operational excellence, supply chain, and process performance.

  • Official VDA QMC Auditor & Trainer (VDA 6.3, 6.5, MLA, PSCR, Core Tools)
  • AIAG Specialist (APQP, PPAP, FMEA, MSA, SPC)
  • Strong competence in IATF16949:2016, ISO 9001 & ISO 14001
  • Experience managing quality requirements with major OEMs
